Chasing Sunsets in Bali

August 2025

There’s nothing quite like a Bali sunset. The sky turns orange and pink, surfers catch their last waves, and everything slows down.

This trip took me from Ubud’s rice terraces to Uluwatu’s cliffs — and every spot had something magical.

Favorite moments? Floating in a waterfall at sunrise and sharing nasi goreng with locals.
