# Redwood National Park

### Where to Stay

- **Gold Beach**  
  <small>*Charming home with ocean views and easy access to local trails.*</small>  

- **Trinidad**  
  <small>*Cozy cabin near the coast, perfect for exploring nearby beaches and parks.*</small>  

### What to do

- **Big Trees Trail (permit required)**  
  <small>*Walk among ancient redwoods on this iconic trail.*</small>

- **Klamath River Overlook**  
  <small>*Scenic views of the Klamath River meeting the Pacific Ocean.*</small>

- **Trinidad State Beach**  
  <small>*Tide pools, beach walks, and stunning sunsets.*</small>

- **Jedediah Smith Redwoods State Park - Grove of Titans Trailhead**  
  <small>*Explore one of the most impressive redwood groves in the park.*</small>

- **Meyers Creek Beach**  
  <small>*A quiet beach, ideal for picnics and coastal walks.*</small>

### Skip

- **Trees of Mystery**  
  <small>*Touristy and often crowded; not recommended if you prefer quiet nature walks.*</small>
