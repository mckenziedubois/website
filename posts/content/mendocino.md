# Mendocino

### Where to Stay

- **Stanford Inn By the Sea**  
  <small>*Dog friendly, ocean views.*</small>

### What to do

- **Catch a Canoe**  
  <small>*Rent a outrigger canoe and explore the serene Mendocino waterways, perfect for wildlife spotting.*</small>
- **Mendocino Cliffs Trail**  
  <small>*Hike along dramatic coastal cliffs with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean.*</small>
- **Russian Gulch State Park**  
  <small>*Explore forests, waterfalls, and rugged coastline on scenic trails.*</small>
- **Navarro Beach**  
  <small>*A quieter beach ideal for sunset walks and tide pooling.*</small>

### Where to eat

- **MacCallum House**  
  <small>*Enjoy farm-to-table dishes with a focus on local ingredients.*</small>
- **The Brickery**  
  <small>*Casual spot offering wood-fired pizzas and hearty comfort food.*</small>
- **Raven's Restaurant (Vegan)**  
  <small>*A favorite for creative vegan dishes in a relaxed setting.*</small>
