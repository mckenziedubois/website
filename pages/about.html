About Me

In the world of travel planning, my journeys often begin with lists, books, texts, maps, notes, google docs, and spreadsheets created by myself and others. When close friends and families ask for advice or recommendations I have too much information to send and from too many different sources. I needed some place to document my travels outside of social media that would allow me to compile all this information. That and given my experience in web development, I decided to create my own site.
